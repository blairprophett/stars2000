Email Diane @ dkstars2000@yahoo.com to secure your appointment time.

All performances at the Las Lomas High School Theatre, 1460 S. Main St., Walnut Creek

Please prepare a contemporary song of 32 bars or less from a musical in the style of the show. Songs should highlight personality and voice. Bring sheet music in the correct key. An accompanist will be provided.

All auditionees will be taught a short dance combination. Please dress comfortably in something you can move in that shows the line of your body (i.e. no baggy pants, oversized sweaters or tees).

Bring dance shoes (i.e. no flip flops, stilettos or Uggs).

What Our Actors Say!

"STARS provided me with some of the most memorable summers of my life. The connections I made and the hands-on training were undeniable. I was given the opportunity to develop characters in full productions, which forced me to build a work ethic that has followed me to this day. The act of "putting on a show" is a precious experience that can be very difficult to teach. STARS does what no other teen program in the area can do. The experienced instructors assemble a company of young individuals and provides them with the necessary guidance and resources. What soon becomes apparent to every newcomer is that the entire ensemble must work together in order to make it to opening night."

~Tony Conaty
